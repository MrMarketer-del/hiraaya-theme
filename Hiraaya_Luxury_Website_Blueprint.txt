LOADING GOOGLE FONTS (WORDPRESS)
In WordPress, load both fonts via Appearance > Customize > Additional CSS or enqueue in functions.php: @import url('https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Cormorant+Garamond:ital,wght@0,400;0,600;1,400&family=Jost:wght@300;400;500&display=swap');

Signature Design Element
The single most distinctive visual element on the Hiraaya website will be a custom Indian architectural line illustration used as a watermark-opacity background layer in hero sections and the footer. This could be a Mughal arch, the Gateway of India in line art, or a Rajasthan haveli silhouette rendered as a thin gold SVG at 8-12% opacity. No competitor in this space uses this device. It signals cultural rootedness without being decorative or clichéd.
ILLUSTRATION DIRECTION
Commission a freelance illustrator to produce 2-3 architectural line-art SVGs: one arch/gateway, one palace skyline, one interior archway. These will be reused across Home hero, About hero, and the footer. Budget: one-time asset.

04 | PAGE-BY-PAGE LAYOUT BLUEPRINTS
Home Page
WORDPRESS TEMPLATE: FRONT PAGE
Section 1 — Navigation (Sticky Header)
The navigation bar is transparent on load and transitions to Cherry Deep (#3D0D17) on scroll. Logo: the H monogram + HIRAAYA LUXURY wordmark in Cormorant Garamond, gold. Links: About, Services, Sectors, The Perspective, Contact — Jost Light, uppercase, tracked, ivory. No dropdown menus on desktop. On mobile: collapse to H monogram + hamburger icon, open full-screen overlay in cherry-deep with gold links.

Section 2 — Hero
Full-bleed height: 100vh. Background: dark photographic image of an Indian heritage interior or archway, with a deep cherry-red colour overlay at 70% opacity. Centred content:
	•	Eyebrow text: LUXURY BRAND MANAGEMENT (gold, tracked caps, Jost)
	•	Headline (H1): Where the path of refinement is illuminated by luxury (Cormorant Garamond, ivory, 64-72px desktop)

	•	CTA Button: Discover Hiraaya (outlined, gold border, gold text, no fill — hover fills gold, text turns cherry)
	•	Scroll indicator: thin vertical line with a downward-moving dot in gold at bottom centre

HERO WORDPRESS NOTES
Use Elementor Full-Height section. Background image layer + colour overlay layer stacked. Animate headline text with Elementor Motion Effects: fade-up on load, 0.3s delay. For the scroll indicator, use a custom HTML widget with a small CSS animation.

05 | CONTENT VOICE & WRITING RULES
How Hiraaya Sounds on the Website
Every word on the Hiraaya site is design material. The copy must be as deliberate as the colour palette. The following rules govern all web copy — for headers, body text, CTAs, form labels, and even error messages.

Words to Use
	•	Refinement, presence, perception, enduring, cultivate
	•	Collaborators or partners (not clients)
	•	Engagements (not projects or campaigns)
	•	Experience-led, relationship-driven, culturally aware
	•	Strategic, considered, long-term, thoughtful

Words to Avoid Entirely
	•	Agency, full-service agency, digital marketing, social media
	•	Campaigns, deliverables, metrics, ROI, conversions
	•	Client (use collaborator or partner), portfolio (use selected engagements)
	•	Leverage, synergies, comprehensive solutions, end-to-end
	•	Delve, holistic, game-changer, cutting-edge, innovative

Sentence Style Rules
	•	Short and declarative in hero sections: one idea per sentence
	•	No bullet lists in About or hero sections — prose only
	•	Active voice throughout: never 'luxury is cultivated by us' — always 'we cultivate luxury'
	•	No em dashes anywhere on the site
	•	No AI-sounding language or filler phrases
	•	Sentence case for all headings — never all-caps headings in body content (eyebrows excepted)
	•	Edit every sentence for weight: if it can be cut, cut it

Tone Adjectives
Considered — not showy. Warm — not cold. Authoritative — not arrogant. Indian-rooted and globally fluent. Philosophical — not academic.
